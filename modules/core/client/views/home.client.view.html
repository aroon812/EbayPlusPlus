Start Here!

You can navagate this app by using the links on the top navigation bar.

- The "ebaY++" icon takes you to this page

- The "Chat" button takes you to an in app messaging room for buyer/seller communication

- The "Me" drop button takes you to a page that displays your current bids and recent purchases

- The "Items" drop button takes you to a page that displays current items for sale

- The "Profile" drop button takes you to a settings page for your personal profile
